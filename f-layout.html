Celtics With the Sweep

The Celtics end up with a very convincing sweep of the Indiana Pacers. They are looking like the title contenders that everyone thought they would be at the beginning of the season. Next they face the real challenge in round number 2 of the NBA Playoffs.

Next Up the Milwaukee Bucks

The bucks are the #1 overall seed in the NBA and look like they will sweep their round one series against the Orlando Magic. They are favorites to represent the Eastern Conference in the finals so the Celtics have a tough task ahead. In order to beat this high-powered team, the Celtics have to play their best basketball yet.
